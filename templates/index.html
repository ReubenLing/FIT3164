Water Management in Dairy Farms

south

Despite being the industry that consumes the most water in Australia, there exists a lack of awareness and urgency in improving methods of obtaining, storing and using water within the dairy industry. As about a third of irrigated land in the country is occupied by dairy farms, it is imperative that awareness and incentives for more efficient and sustainable measures are brought to light.

Our key goal is to use data visualisation to inform businesses in the dairy industry on practices to apply within their organisation in order to reduce their water consumption. This will hold benefits to the environment and society as it could reduce the natural resources needed to run Australiaâ€™s largest agricultural sector.
